## Summary

**Aeremore** is a renowned centre for magical learning and scholarly pursuit within the continent of [[Places/Continents/Jealeon/index|Jealeon]]. Built into a mountain, the city features [[Places/Continents/Jealeon/Regions/Aeremore/Magleth Manor|Magleth Manor]] at its summit. [[Places/Continents/Jealeon/Regions/Aeremore|Aeremore]] is the campaign's primary destination.
