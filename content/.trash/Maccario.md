## Overview

[[People/NPCs/Maccario|Maccario]] is a dwarven official at the [[Organisations/Aeremore Adventurers' League|Aeremore Adventurers' League]] in [[Places/Continents/Jealeon/Regions/Aeremore|Aeremore]]. He manages contracts and entrance tests for new adventurers.

## Recent Developments

- [[Notes/Session 8|Session 8]]: Offered the party a contract to investigate stolen plans and oversaw their registration as [[Organisations/The Prime Suspects|The Prime Suspects]].

## Role

- Responsible for vetting new members and assigning contracts.
