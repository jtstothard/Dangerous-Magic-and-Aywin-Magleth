One half of a formerly unified nation within the continent of [[Places/Continents/Jealeon/index|Jealeon]], now separated from [[West Meashelles]] due to financial disparity.

## Overview

**East Meashelles** represents the eastern portion of what was once a single nation. The region split from [[West Meashelles]] due to significant financial and economic disparities that made unified governance untenable.
