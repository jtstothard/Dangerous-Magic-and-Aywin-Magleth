## Overview

**Aswen** is a politically significant region that stands in stark opposition to its neighbour [[Places/Continents/Jealeon/Regions/Aswesh/index|Aswesh]]. The two regions harbour intense mutual hatred, though the specific historical reasons for this animosity remain unclear to outsiders.
