One half of a formerly unified nation within the continent of [[Jealeon|Jealeon]], now separated from [[East Meashelles]] due to financial disparity.

## Overview

**West Meashelles** represents the western portion of what was once a single nation. The region split from [[East Meashelles]] due to significant financial and economic disparities that made unified governance untenable.
