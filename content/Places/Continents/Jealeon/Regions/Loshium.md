## Overview

**Loshium** represents one of the Life domain nations within [[Places/Continents/Jealeon/index|Jealeon]], noted for its peaceful nature and recent conflicts with [[Spuaburg]]. It is characterised by a strong association with the Life Domain deity [[Zurasus]], with notable religious group "The Pacifiers" being a significant part of its cultural identity.
