A region within the continent of [[Places/Continents/Jealeon/index|Jealeon]], known for its strict border controls and ongoing political tensions with neighboring [[Aswen]].

## Overview

**Aswesh** is a politically significant region that maintains careful control over its borders, requiring thorough inspection of all belongings for travelers entering from other regions. This suggests either security concerns or potential smuggling issues affecting the area.

## Geography and Settlements

### Towns
- **[[Places/Continents/Jealeon/Regions/Aswesh/Towns/Cerbereburn/index|Cerbereburn]]**: A small rural town currently dealing with murder mysteries and supernatural threats (werechickens, werewolves)

## Political Situation

### Border Security
- **Strict Border Controls**: All travelers entering Aswesh from other regions must submit to thorough searches of their belongings
- **Security Concerns**: The intensive border checks suggest either smuggling concerns or political tensions requiring careful monitoring

### Regional Conflicts
- **Tensions with [[Aswen]]**: The two regions "hate each other" according to local knowledge, though the specific reasons for this animosity remain unclear
- **Recent Activity**: Border guards have been particularly vigilant, as experienced by travelers like [[Gralstak]] who faced questioning about his alchemical supplies

## Current Events

The region is currently dealing with:
- **Murder investigations** in [[Places/Continents/Jealeon/Regions/Aswesh/Towns/Cerbereburn/index|Cerbereburn]]
- **Supernatural threats** including lycanthrope activity
- **Political tensions** related to land disputes and external influence from groups like the Keggronites

## Travel Notes

- **Entry Requirements**: Expect thorough searches at border checkpoints
- **Political Sensitivity**: Visitors should be aware of the ongoing tensions with [[Aswen]]
- **Current Instability**: Some towns are in lockdown due to recent violent incidents
