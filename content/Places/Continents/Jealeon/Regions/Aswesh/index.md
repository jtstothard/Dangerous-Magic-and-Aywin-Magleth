A region within the continent of [[Places/Continents/Jealeon/index|Jealeon]], known for its strict border controls and ongoing political tensions with neighbouring [[Aswen]].

## Overview

**Aswesh** is a politically significant region that maintains careful control over its borders, requiring thorough inspection of all belongings for travellers entering from other regions. This suggests either security concerns or potential smuggling issues affecting the area.
