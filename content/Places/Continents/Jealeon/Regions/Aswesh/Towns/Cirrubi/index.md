## Overview

Cirrubi is a prominent town in Aswesh, Jealeon, known for its castle, river, and cosmopolitan atmosphere. The party arrived here after their encounter with Snadneg and the mercenaries, staying at The Empty Apple Tavern.

Cirrubi is the former capital of [[Places/Continents/Jealeon/Regions/Aswesh/index|Aswesh]], under King [[People/NPCs/Harkins|Harkins]]. It was replaced as capital by [[Places/Continents/Jealeon/Regions/Aswesh/Towns/Oreon|Oreon]] under King [[People/NPCs/Albert Rathbone|Albert Rathbone]].

Site of the [[Organisations/Cirrubi Central Bureaucracy|Central Bureaucracy]].

## Notable Locations

- [[The Empty Apple Tavern]]: Popular inn and tavern where the party stayed during Session 14.
- Castle and castle grounds: Central landmark atop a hill, surrounded by walls and a flowing river.

## Recent Events

- [[Notes/Session 14|Session 14]]: The party arrived, disguised themselves with Seeming, navigated local tensions, and referenced Bear Cloudstripe as a teleportation hub.
