## **Building Summary**

**Cloudbrow Farm & Barn** is the agricultural heart of the Cloudbrow family operations and the epicentre of the supernatural lycanthropy outbreak that has terrorised [[Places/Continents/Jealeon/Regions/Aswesh/Towns/Cerbereburn/index|Cerbereburn]]. This working farm has become a crime scene where werechickens emerged and the beloved [[Mrs Cluckerson]] mysteriously disappeared.

---

### **Physical Description**

- **Architecture**: Traditional farming complex with main barn and outbuildings
- **Barn Security**: Locked structure with keys held by [[Narchis Cloudbrow|Narchis]] and [[Risgex Cloudbrow]]
- **Livestock Areas**: Chicken coops and various animal enclosures
- **Agricultural Fields**: Working farmland surrounding the main buildings

## **The Lycanthropy Outbreak**

### **Werechicken Incident**

- **Location**: Primary outbreak occurred within the barn
- **Infection Pattern**: Multiple chickens transformed into werechickens
- **Bite Evidence**: Some chickens showed bite marks from a larger creature
- **Cross-Infection**: Most chickens infected by other werechickens rather than original source

### **[[Mrs Cluckerson]] Mystery**

- **Status**: Missing before the outbreak began
- **Significance**: [[Narchis Cloudbrow|Narchis]]'s prized chicken, beloved by the family
- **Timeline**: Disappeared prior to lycanthropy incident
- **Investigation Priority**: Key to understanding outbreak origins

---

## **Security Breach**

### **Lock-Picking Evidence**

- **Method**: Barn lock was picked prior to outbreak
- **Timing**: Break-in occurred before lycanthropy incident
- **Implications**: Suggests deliberate release rather than natural outbreak
- **Investigation**: Clear evidence of criminal intent

### **Access Control**

- **Key Holders**: Only [[Narchis Cloudbrow|Narchis]] and [[Risgex Cloudbrow]] had legitimate access
- **Security Failure**: Lock-picking indicates outside interference
- **Crime Scene**: Barn entrance shows signs of tampering
