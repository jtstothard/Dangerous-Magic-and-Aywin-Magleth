## Summary

Cerbereburn is a small rural town marked by political conflict, land disputes, and distrust of outsiders. The town is recovering from a recent werewolf and werechicken crisis, with tensions still high and several mysteries unresolved.

## Notable Locations

- [[The Owlbear Cub Tavern]]–Social hub
- [[Mallone Ulaxidor's Home]]–Former lawmaster's residence
- [[Greyson Tools]]–Blacksmith shop
- [[Tallbloom Stables]]–Horse stables
- [[Brightroot Apartments]]–Multi-tenant building
- [[Cloudbrow Farm & Barn]]–Farm and outbreak site
