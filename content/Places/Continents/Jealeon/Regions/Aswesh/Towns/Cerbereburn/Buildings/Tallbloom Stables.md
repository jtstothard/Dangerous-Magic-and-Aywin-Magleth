## **Building Summary**

**Tallbloom Stables** is the town's primary horse and livestock facility, owned and operated by the [[Saria Tallbloom|Tallbloom family]]. Located downstream from [[Greyson Tools]], the stables have become both a victim of environmental pollution and a centre of family tensions during the current crisis.

---

## **Physical Description**

- **Architecture**: Traditional stable construction with multiple stalls and paddocks
- **Location**: Downstream from [[Greyson Tools]] along the river
- **Water Access**: River water supply affected by upstream pollution
- **Family Residence**: Connected to or adjacent to Tallbloom Home
