## **Building Summary**

**Greyson Tools** is [[Bertrum Greyson]]'s blacksmith workshop, a vital business in [[Places/Continents/Jealeon/Regions/Aswesh/Towns/Cerbereburn/index|Cerbereburn]] that has become the centre of significant environmental and political controversy. The shop's location along the river and its forging operations have made it a focal point in the town's resource disputes.

---

## **Physical Description**

- **Location**: Positioned along the river for easy access to water for forging operations
- **River Access**: Direct connection to the waterway
- **Downstream Impact**: Located upstream from [[Tallbloom Stables]], causing pollution concerns

## **Environmental Controversy**

### **River Pollution Dispute**

- **Source of Conflict**: Forge operations accused of polluting the river water
- **Primary Complainant**: [[Saria Tallbloom]] from downstream [[Tallbloom Stables]]
- **Impact**: Pollution allegedly affecting horses and livestock downstream
- **[[Bertrum Greyson]] Response**: Grumpy and defensive about accusations
