## Overview

Cirrubi is a bustling city in the region of Aeremore, Jealeon, known for its labyrinthine bureaucracy, tense political climate, and history as the former capital of Jealeon. The city has been the site of ongoing investigations, magical incidents, and party intrigue.

## Recent Events

- The party infiltrated Cirrubi using forged documents and quick thinking. See: [[Notes/Session 15|Session 15]]
- Investigated the [[Organisations/Cirrubi Central Bureaucracy|Cirrubi Central Bureaucracy]], where they were accused of stealing plans and faced suspicion from guards. See: [[Notes/Session 15|Session 15]], [[Notes/Session 16|Session 16]]
- The city was the backdrop for undead attacks, magical theft, and dramatic escapes. See: [[Notes/Session 16|Session 16]], [[Notes/Session 17|Session 17]]
- Party members were arrested and detained at the police station, leading to a tense and dangerous escape. See: [[Notes/Session 17|Session 17]]

## Key Locations

- [[Organisations/Cirrubi Central Bureaucracy|Cirrubi Central Bureaucracy]]: The heart of the city's administration and the site of major investigations.
- Police station: Where the party was detained and questioned.

## Notable NPCs

- City Guards: Suspicious of the party due to the archive incident.
- Bureaucracy staff: Involved in the investigation and questioning of the party.
