## Overview

Outer Osterford is a district in [[Places/Continents/Jealeon/Regions/Aeremore/index|Aeremore]], known for its mix of working-class neighbourhoods and newer developments. It is the home of [[Jezebel Milhaud]] and [[Abel Milhaud]] as of [[Notes/Session 9|Session 9]].

## Recent Developments

- [[Notes/Session 9|Session 9]]: Location of [[Abel Milhaud]]'s disappearance and the party's investigation. [[Abel Milhaud]] and [[Jezebel Milhaud]] reside here.
