## Overview

Magleth Manor is the legendary residence of the powerful elven wizard [[People/NPCs/Legendary/Aywin Magleth|Aywin Magleth]], located somewhere within the region of [[Places/Continents/Jealeon/Regions/Aeremore/index|Aeremore]]. While the specific location within Aeremore remains secretive, befitting the nature of its owner, the manor serves as both a residence and likely a centre of magical research and operations.

## Architectural Plans

The architectural plans for Magleth Manor were stored in the [[Organisations/Cirrubi Central Bureaucracy|Cirrubi Central Bureaucracy]] but were mysteriously stolen by individuals matching the descriptions of [[Organisations/Adventuring Parties/The Prime Suspects|The Prime Suspects]]. [[People/NPCs/Legendary/Aywin Magleth|Magleth]] himself claims no responsibility for this theft, suggesting someone else orchestrated the heist using the party's likenesses.

The theft of these plans indicates:

- The manor's design contains significant strategic or magical value
- Someone wishes to study or potentially infiltrate the manor
- The plans may reveal defensive measures or magical protections
- The theft was carefully planned to implicate specific individuals

## Magical Properties

Given [[People/NPCs/Legendary/Aywin Magleth|Magleth]]'s reputation and abilities, the manor likely possesses:

- Advanced magical defences and wards
- Teleportation capabilities (as evidenced by Magleth's transportation methods)
- Illusion magic integrated into its structure
- Scrying and divination protections
- Potentially extra-dimensional spaces or properties
