## Description

- Location: [[Places/Continents/Jealeon/Regions/Aeremore/index|Aeremore]], [[Inner Osterford]].
- Purpose: A prestigious research facility known for its magical studies and experiments.
- Appearance: A grand stone building with intricate carvings and magical wards.

## Recent Developments

- **[[Notes/Session 10|Session 10]]**:
  - The party infiltrated the Pashae Institute to locate [[Abel Milhaud]].
  - [[Thanatos]] scouted the building invisibly, discovering an abandoned kitchen and a locked room where [[Abel Milhaud|Abel]] was restrained.
  - The party triggered a magical trap, activating animated kitchenware, and fought enchanted cutlery and an oven.
  - [[Abel Milhaud]] was rescued, though the purpose of the experiments conducted on him remains unclear.

- **[[Notes/Session 11|Session 11]]**:
  - The party rescued [[Abel Milhaud]], who was found pale and weak, with puncture marks indicating blood theft. The room where he was held contained vials of unknown substances, suggesting ongoing experiments.
  - The institute's mages engaged the party in combat, using potions that enhanced their speed and resilience but caused visible physical degradation. One mage escaped using invisibility, while the other was killed. Upon death, the mage's body exploded with arcane energy.
  - The party successfully escaped with Abel, but the institute's response to this intrusion remains uncertain.
