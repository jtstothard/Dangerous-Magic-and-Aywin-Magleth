## Overview

Inner Osterford is a district in [[Places/Continents/Jealeon/Regions/Aeremore/index|Aeremore]], known for its busier streets and more central location compared to [[Outer Osterford]]. It is a common area for public gatherings and performances.

## Recent Developments

- [[Notes/Session 9|Session 9]]: [[Abel Milhaud]] was seen busking with magic here prior to his disappearance.
