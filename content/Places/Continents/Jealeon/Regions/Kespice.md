## Overview

**Kespice** is a coastal region that serves as an important transit hub for travellers seeking to move between different areas of [[Places/Continents/Jealeon/index|Jealeon]]. The region provides sea-based alternatives to overland travel, particularly useful for those seeking to avoid the political tensions of land borders.
