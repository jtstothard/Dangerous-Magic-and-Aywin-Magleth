## Summary

**Aeremore** is a renowned centre for magical learning and scholarly pursuit within the continent of [[Places/Continents/Jealeon/index|index]]. Built into a mountain, the city features Magleth Manor at its summit. Aeremore is the campaign's primary destination.

## Notable Locations

- Magleth Manor (at the top of the city)
- The Cobbles (outside Magleth Manor)
- The Pentacores
- Inner Osterford (outside the Pentacores)
- Outer Osterford (outside the city walls)

## Current Events

- Outer Osterford has expanded beyond the city walls as more building was required.
