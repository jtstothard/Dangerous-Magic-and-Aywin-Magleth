The campaign's primary destination, **Aeremore** is a renowned center for magical learning and scholarly pursuit within the continent of [[Places/Continents/Jealeon/index|Jealeon]].

## Overview

Aeremore serves as a hub for adventurers, scholars, and magic practitioners who come to study arcane arts and uncover mystical truths. The city attracts those seeking to advance their magical knowledge and understanding of the world's deeper mysteries.

## Significance to the Campaign

- **Destination for [[Lyra Shadowflame]]**: She travels here specifically to study magic and expand her arcane knowledge
- **Destination for [[Gralstak]]**: He seeks to find strong allies in this center of learning
- **Destination for the Party**: Multiple party members have reasons to visit, making it a natural convergence point for their adventures

## Political Context

Aeremore appears to be in the midst of, or recently affected by, political tensions involving the neighboring regions of [[Places/Continents/Jealeon/Regions/Aswesh/index|Aswesh]] and [[Aswen]]. The party's journey here involves understanding "truths around why the two peace nations split," suggesting Aeremore may hold important information about recent regional conflicts.

## Access and Travel

Travelers commonly approach Aeremore through various routes:

- Overland travel through [[Places/Continents/Jealeon/Regions/Aswesh/index|Aswesh]] (requiring border checks)
- Sea routes from [[Kespice]] (though some vessels may be pirate ships)
- Various other regional connections across [[Places/Continents/Jealeon/index|Jealeon]]
