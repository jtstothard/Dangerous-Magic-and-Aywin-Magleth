## Lumbadar

**Lumbadar** is the first of the three moons that orbit the world. It follows a 28-day lunar cycle and has significant connections to lycanthropic transformations.

### Astronomical Properties

- **Lunar Cycle:** 28 days
- **Position:** First moon in the celestial system

### Lycanthropic Connections

Based on investigations during the [[Places/Continents/Jealeon/Regions/Aswesh/Towns/Cerbereburn/index|Cerbereburn]] incidents:

- Each lycanthrope is tied to a specific moon for their transformation cycle
- Full moon phases cause forced transformations in affected individuals
- Understanding moon phases is crucial for predicting lycanthropic activity
