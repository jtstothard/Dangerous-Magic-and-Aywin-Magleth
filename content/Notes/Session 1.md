## **The Murder Discovery**

*Morning brings tragedy and suspicion to the small town.*

- **The Crime Scene:** [[Mallone Ulaxidor|Mallone]] found dead in the street with a stab wound, notably no blood spatter present
- **First Responder:** [[Lharamit Pirevin Rhyne|Lharamit]] investigated the body, drawing accusations from townspeople of "returning to the scene"
- **Key Details:**
  - [[Mallone Ulaxidor|Mallone]] had been dead for hours
  - Her rapier remained with the body, ruling out robbery
  - No signs she returned home after leaving the tavern
- **New Authority:** [[Marius Ligroves|Marius]], a nervous gnome deputy, left in charge
- **Town Response:** Immediate lockdown initiated, party volunteers to investigate

## **Investigation Highlights**

### **Lharamit & Manmosu**

- Investigated [[Mallone Ulaxidor|Mallone]]'s house
- [[Manmosu]] tried to break in, [[Lharamit Pirevin Rhyne|Lharamit]] got the key
- Found [[Mallone Ulaxidor|Mallone]]'s journal: notes about local disputes:
  - Pollution feud between [[Bertrum Greyson|Bertrum]] (blacksmith) & [[Saria Tallbloom|Saria]] (stables)
  - Keggronites buying land, especially [[Strondabella Keggranite|Strondabella]]
  - Local farmer [[Narchis Cloudbrow|Narchis]] suspicious of Keggronites
  - [[Dhunta Rannacit]]: general complainer, disliked by [[Mallone Ulaxidor|Mallone]]

### **Robin & Gralstak**

- Investigated the tiefling [[Risgara Geraves|Risgara]]
- [[Gralstak]] barged in, [[Robin Thaleia|Robin]] used *Charm Person* and later *Sleep*
- His house was messy, nothing suspicious, cutlery unused

### **Lyra & Gralstak**

- Questioned [[Bertrum Greyson|Bertrum]] the blacksmith
- [[Lyra Shadowflame|Lyra]] recreated the murder weapon with *Minor Illusion*
- [[Bertrum Greyson|Bertrum]] couldn't identify it. [[Gralstak]]'s intimidation failed

### **Threecie & Manmosu**

- Questioned [[Dhunta Rannacit|Dhunta]] (accuser)
- Complained about farms, land purchases, and town decline
- Suspects Keggronites. Her wife [[Javu Rannacit|Javu]] was polite but quiet

### **[[Robin Thaleia|Robin]] & [[Lharamit Pirevin Rhyne|Lharamit]]: The Keggronites**

- **Subjects:** [[Jafrak Keggranite|Jafrak]] and [[Strondabella Keggranite|Strondabella]]
- **[[Jafrak Keggranite|Jafrak]]'s Response:** Suspected the [[Risgara Geraves|Risgara]], maintained politeness but remained evasive
- **[[Strondabella Keggranite|Strondabella]]'s Behaviour:** Watchful and wary throughout the interaction
- **Strategic Interest:** [[Lharamit Pirevin Rhyne|Lharamit]] showed interest in their documents, [[Robin Thaleia|Robin]] hinted at potential covert access but expressed uncertainty
