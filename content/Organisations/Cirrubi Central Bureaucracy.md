## Overview

The Central Bureaucracy is the main administrative body of [[Places/Continents/Jealeon/Regions/Aswesh/Towns/Cirrubi|Cirrubi]], formerly the capital of [[Places/Continents/Jealeon/Regions/Aswesh/index|Aswesh]].

## Recent Developments

- [[Notes/Session 12|Session 12]]: Architectural plans for [[Places/Continents/Jealeon/Regions/Aeremore/Buildings/Magleth Manor|Magleth Manor]] were reportedly stolen from the bureaucracy. The theft was allegedly committed by people matching [[The Prime Suspects]]' descriptions, though [[People/NPCs/Legendary/Aywin Magleth|Aywin Magleth]] claims no responsibility for either the theft or the use of their likenesses.
