## Summary

The Pashae Institute is a prestigious research organisation in Aeremore, known for its cutting-edge magical studies and experiments. It is a hub for magical innovation but has recently come under scrutiny due to mysterious activities.

## Recent Developments

- **[[Notes/Session 10|Session 10]]**:
  - The party infiltrated the Pashae Institute to locate Abel Milhaud.
  - Evidence of magical experiments was discovered, including enchanted kitchenware.
  - Abel Milhaud was rescued, though the purpose of the experiments remains unclear.

- **[[Notes/Session 11|Session 11]]**:
  - The party rescued Abel Milhaud, who was found pale and weak, with puncture marks indicating blood theft. The room contained vials of unknown substances, suggesting ongoing experiments.
  - The institute's mages engaged the party in combat, using potions that enhanced their speed and resilience but caused visible physical degradation. One mage escaped using invisibility, while the other was killed. Upon death, the mage's body exploded with arcane energy.
  - The party successfully escaped with Abel, but the institute's response to this intrusion remains uncertain.
