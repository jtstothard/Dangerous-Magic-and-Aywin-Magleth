**Appearance:** Translucent blue scales, **glass left eye**, **scarring on left side**, always keeps **left arm covered**. When uncovered, the arm is found to be spectral

## **Character Overview**

- **Class:** Celestial Warlock (INT-based)
- **Personality:** Quiet, precise, and analytical. Speaks bluntly when distracted. Often uses telepathy, sometimes forgets and speaks aloud.
- **Notable Traits:**
	- Former **mortician and healer**, trained in ritual and care
	- Deeply affected by family tragedy, but emotionally composed
	- Magic comes from a place of light and service, despite her solemn vibe

---

## **Background**

- Grew up in **Oreon, [[Places/Continents/Jealeon/Regions/Aswesh/index|Aswesh]]**, working in the family funeral business
- Moved to **[[Aeremore]]** two years ago after an incident that killed her twin brother and left her father in a soul-separated coma
- On a job in Cerbereburn for her mercenary group, travelling with Lharamit
