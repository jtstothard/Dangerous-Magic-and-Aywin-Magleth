## Basic Information

- **Species:** Human (with greenish hue)
- **Physical description:** 7'4", bald, greenish skin, ragged clothes, monocle, always carries potions and a massive ornate greataxe
- **Key relationships:** [[Lyra Shadowflame]] (travel companion), [[Manmosu]] (party member), [[Robin Thaleia]] (party member), [[Marius Ligroves]] (frequent target of intimidation)

## Character Overview

- **Class:** Possibly Barbarian or Alchemist/Artificer hybrid
- **Personality traits:** Blunt, impulsive, rough around the edges, disregards social etiquette, self-conscious about appearance
- **Notable equipment/companions:** Potions, monocle, massive ornate greataxe, fart jar that records sound

## Background

- **Origins:** From [[Aswen]] seeking strong allies for a personal mission
- **Goals:** Find allies, solve local crises
- **Connections to other characters:** Travels with [[Lyra Shadowflame]], interacts with all party members, often at odds with authority figures
