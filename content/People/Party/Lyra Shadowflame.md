## Lyra Shadowflame

**Lyra Shadowflame** is a 5'8" tiefling cleric/wizard with a bookish demeanor and scholarly pursuits. She wears well-worn librarian-style clothes, maintains a hunched posture, and always carries a massive tome. Her most notable companion is her owl familiar, [[Hooty]].

### Basic Information

- **Pronouns:** She/Her
- **Species:** Tiefling
- **Height:** 5'8"
- **Class:** Cleric/Wizard
- **Personality:** Bookish, observant, direct
- **Familiar:** [[Hooty]] the owl

### Appearance & Equipment

- Wears well-worn librarian-style clothes
- Hunched posture from carrying heavy books
- Always carries a massive tome
- Accompanied by her watchful owl familiar [[Hooty]]

### Background

- **Destination:** Traveling to [[Aeremore]] to study magic at a formal institution
- **Travel Companion:** Met [[Gralstak]] on the road; tolerates him but takes leadership when needed
- **Faith:** From a family that follows the Peace Domain and worships [[Rainia, the Winged Harmoniser]]

### Investigation & Combat Experience

#### Magic Use in Investigation

- **Minor Illusion Expertise:** Successfully recreated the murder weapon to test [[Bertrum Greyson]]'s knowledge (though he claimed not to recognize it)
- **Attempted Misdirection:** Tried using *Minor Illusion* to distract [[Risgex Cloudbrow]] but was caught, raising suspicions about the party's magical activities
- **Silvery Barbs Interference:** Used *Silvery Barbs* on [[Lharamit Pirevin Rhyne|Lharamit]]'s attack during werechicken combat, accidentally causing [[Lharamit Pirevin Rhyne|Lharamit]] to blast [[Manmosu]] instead

#### Combat & Tactical Decisions

- **Werechicken Combat:** Felt guilty about sabotaging a party member's attack and considered extreme solutions like arson
- **Barn Stakeout:** Partnered with [[Gralstak]] to stake out the Cloudbrow barn where they discovered the werechicken outbreak
- **Stealth Challenges:** Showed inexperience with stealth during barn surveillance, struggling with proper concealment

#### Social Interactions & Investigation

- **Relationship Tensions:** Felt disliked by [[Risgex Cloudbrow]] (while [[Gralstak]] thought the opposite)
- **Legal Troubles:** Was arrested along with [[Manmosu]] by [[Marius Ligroves]] and [[Strondabella Keggranite]] to ensure party cooperation
- **Surveillance Work:** Worked effectively with [[Gralstak]] during night watch and found flattened grass evidence near the apartments

#### Familiar Management

- **[[Hooty]] Coordination:** Effectively uses her owl familiar for reconnaissance, particularly during the barn incident
- **Tactical Communication:** Shows both humor and practical thinking about familiar versatility in various situations
- **Evidence Gathering:** Participates in searching for evidence and takes shifts watching locations of interest
