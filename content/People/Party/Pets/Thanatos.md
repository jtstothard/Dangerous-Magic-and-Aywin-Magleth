## Overview

Thanatos is the raven-like familiar of [[People/Party/Threecie Novik|Threecie Novik]]. Thanatos appeared to [[Threecie Novik|Threecie]] in a dream and manifested physically, described as a bird with anatomy that seems wrong, as if reimagined by someone who has never seen a raven.

## Appearance

- Feathers appear made of shadow
- Head opens to speak
- Gold chain on [[Threecie Novik|Threecie]]'s arm, trailing off to nothing
- Red spiral marking on Thanatos's leg
