---

## **Character Overview**

- **Role:** Magical familiar to [[Lyra Shadowflame|Lyra]]
- **Personality:** Observant, helpful, and loyal
- **Notable Traits:**
	- Serves as reconnaissance and early warning system
	- Understands complex instructions and situations
	- Can communicate findings effectively to the party

---

## **Recent Investigation Activities**

**Surveillance and Scouting:**
- **Barn reconnaissance** - Sent by [[Lyra Shadowflame|Lyra]] to scout the Cloudbrow barn during the werechicken outbreak
- **First to spot danger** - Spotted chickens trying to break out and transforming into werechickens
- **Party alert system** - Successfully alerted the rest of the party when [[Lyra Shadowflame|Lyra]] and [[Gralstak]] encountered the werechicken threat

**Combat Support:**
- Present during the werechicken combat but focused on observation and warning rather than direct combat
- Demonstrates tactical awareness by understanding when to fetch help

---

## **Relationship With [[Lyra Shadowflame|Lyra]]**

[[Hooty]] shows a strong bond with [[Lyra Shadowflame|Lyra]], following complex instructions and serving as both companion and magical tool. [[Lyra Shadowflame|Lyra]] has joked about [[Hooty]] potentially needing to "become a fish" during river investigations, showing both their working relationship and her sense of humour.
