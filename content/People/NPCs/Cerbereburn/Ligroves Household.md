The Ligroves Household is referenced in session notes as the residence and family of Marius and Danlas Ligroves. Investigations in [[Notes/Session 3|Session 3]] indicated suspicious documents linking the Keggranite family to the Ligroves Household; the household is a focal point in the Cerbereburn werechicken outbreak investigation. See related people pages: [[People/NPCs/Cerbereburn/Danlas Ligroves|Danlas Ligroves]] and [[People/NPCs/Cerbereburn/Marius Ligroves|Marius Ligroves]].
