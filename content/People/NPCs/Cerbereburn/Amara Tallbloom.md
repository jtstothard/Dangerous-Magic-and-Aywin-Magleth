**Amara Tallbloom** is a young adult (they/them) and child of [[Saria Tallbloom]], who runs the stables in [[Places/Continents/Jealeon/Regions/Aswesh/Towns/Cerbereburn/index|Cerbereburn]]. Amara lives at the Tallbloom Home with their mother and younger sibling [[Syndra Tallbloom]].

# Key Characteristics

- **Protective of family secrets** - Initially lied to investigators about not hearing anything the night of [[Mallone Ulaxidor|Mallone]]'s murder
- **Caught between loyalty and truth** - When intimidated by [[Gralstak]], eventually revealed that [[Shayee Cloudbrow|Shayee]] was visiting [[Syndra Tallbloom|Syndra]] the night of the murder
- **Concerned about family acceptance** - Doesn't want [[Saria Tallbloom|Saria]] to know about [[Syndra Tallbloom|Syndra]]'s relationship with [[Shayee Cloudbrow|Shayee]] due to their mother being heteronormative and unaccepting
- **Observant and alert** - Witnessed the chaos at the blacksmith's when [[Gralstak]] carried off [[Marius Ligroves|Marius]] and quickly warned their mother

# Role in the Investigation

Amara has been a reluctant but ultimately helpful witness in the murder investigation. They initially attempted to protect their family by withholding information, but their testimony about [[Shayee Cloudbrow|Shayee]]'s visit proved to be a crucial piece of the puzzle. Their quick thinking to alert their mother during the confrontation at the blacksmith's shows they remain vigilant about protecting their family's interests.

# Family Dynamics

Amara appears to be the protective middle child, trying to shield both their younger sibling's romantic relationship and their mother from difficult truths. They seem aware of their mother's conservative views and work to maintain family harmony while navigating the complex social dynamics of the murder investigation.
