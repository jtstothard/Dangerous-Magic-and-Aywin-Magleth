## Overview

Deacon Cain is a [[cathedral]] official in [[Places/Continents/Jealeon/Regions/Aeremore/index|Aeremore]], recognised for his expertise in symbology and magical signs. He is referenced by [[Allabyra Crisella]] as the primary authority for investigating unusual symbols and their meanings.
