## Overview

Deacon Cathetos is a [[cathedral]] official in [[Places/Continents/Jealeon/Regions/Aeremore/index|Aeremore]], recognised for his expertise in magical mutations and unusual magical phenomena. He is referenced by [[Allabyra Crisella]] as the primary authority for investigating magical mutations.
