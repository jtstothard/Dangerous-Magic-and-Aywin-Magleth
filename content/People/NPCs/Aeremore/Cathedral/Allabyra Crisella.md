## Overview

Allabyra Crisella is a senior cleric at the [[Places/Continents/Jealeon/Regions/Aeremore/index|Aeremore]] [[Cathedral]], known for her wisdom, calm authority, and dedication to uncovering magical mysteries. She serves as a mentor and superior to [[People/Party/Lharamit Pirevin Rhyne|Lharamit]], guiding investigations into magical symbols, mutations, and unusual phenomena in [[Places/Continents/Jealeon/Regions/Aeremore/index|Aeremore]].
