## Background

[[Jezebel Milhaud]] is a tiefling woodcarver living in [[Outer Osterford]]. She moved there with her son [[Abel Milhaud]] and her ex-husband [[Methuselah Milhaud]], who left soon after. Jezebel reported Abel missing after he began going out at night, returning with unexplained money, and eventually disappeared. She provided information about Abel's recent behaviour and possessions to the party during their investigation in [[Notes/Session 9|Session 9]].

## Recent Developments

- [[Notes/Session 9|Session 9]]: Interviewed by the party regarding [[Abel Milhaud]]'s disappearance. Provided details about Abel's activities, possessions, and family situation.
