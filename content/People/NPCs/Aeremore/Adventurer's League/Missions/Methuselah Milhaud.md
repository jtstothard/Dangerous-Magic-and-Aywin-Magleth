## Background

[[Methuselah Milhaud]] is the estranged father of [[Abel Milhaud]] and ex-husband of [[Jezebel Milhaud]]. He moved to [[Outer Osterford]] with his family but left soon after. He was previously a dockworker and was mentioned as having tried to make money selling volatile magical rocks.
