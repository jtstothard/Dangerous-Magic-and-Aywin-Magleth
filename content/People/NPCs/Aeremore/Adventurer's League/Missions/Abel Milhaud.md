## Basic Information

- **Species:** Tiefling
- **Age:** 16
- **Pronouns:** he/him
- **Family:** Son of [[Jezebel Milhaud]] (mother, woodcarver) and [[Methuselah Milhaud]] (estranged father, dockworker)
- **Home:** [[Outer Osterford]], [[Places/Continents/Jealeon/Regions/Aeremore/index|Aeremore]]

## Background

[[Abel Milhaud]] is a tiefling teenager who moved to [[Outer Osterford]] with his mother. His father left soon after. Abel had been going out at night, returning with unexplained money, and was seen busking with magic in [[Inner Osterford]]. He possessed magical components and a coded note. He has been missing for about two weeks as of [[Notes/Session 9|Session 9]].

## Recent Developments

- [[Notes/Session 9|Session 9]]: Subject of a missing persons investigation. Disappeared from [[Outer Osterford]], leaving behind magical components and a coded note. Last tracked by the party to the [[Pashae Institute]].
- [[Notes/Session 10|Session 10]]:
  - Found unconscious and restrained in the Pashae Institute during the party's investigation.
  - Rescued by the party after a battle with animated kitchenware.
- [[Notes/Session 11|Session 11]]:
  - Rescued by the party, who found him pale and weak, with puncture marks indicating blood theft.
  - The room where he was held contained vials of unknown substances, suggesting ongoing experiments.
  - Brought to the [[Organisations/Adventuring Parties/index|Adventurers' League]] headquarters for safety. His long-term condition remains uncertain.
- [[Notes/Session 12|Session 12]]:
  - Woke weak but coherent, able to provide an account of his ordeal.
  - Revealed the [[Organisations/Pashae Institute|Pashae Institute]] had been forcing him into magical situations (illusions) where he had to cast spells to survive, then draining his blood afterward.
  - Shared a similar magical aura to [[People/Party/Manmosu|Manmosu]], though significantly weakened by his ordeal.
  - Blood samples showed signs of decay from dark magic according to [[People/Party/Gralstak|Gralstak]]'s analysis.

## Notable Possessions (at time of disappearance)

- Magical components: sulphurous, silicate, and phosphorous rocks (used for cantrips), missing lodestone
- Coded note with unknown writing
- Small stash of gold coins

## Status

Rescued and recovering at the [[Organisations/Adventuring Parties/index|Adventurers' League]] headquarters as of [[Notes/Session 12|Session 12]]. Confirmed to have sorcerous abilities similar to [[People/Party/Manmosu|Manmosu]].
