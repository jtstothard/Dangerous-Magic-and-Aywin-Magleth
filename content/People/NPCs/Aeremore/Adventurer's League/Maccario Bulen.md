## Overview

[[Maccario Bulen|Maccario]] is a dwarven official at the [[Organisations/Adventuring Parties/index|Aeremore Adventurers' League]] in [[Places/Continents/Jealeon/Regions/Aeremore/index|Aeremore]]. He manages contracts and entrance tests for new adventurers.

## Recent Developments

- [[Notes/Session 8|Session 8]]: Offered the party a contract to investigate stolen plans and oversaw their registration as [[The Prime Suspects]].
- [[Notes/Session 9|Session 9]]: Assigned the party the contract to find [[Abel Milhaud]] and provided background on the case.
- [[Notes/Session 12|Session 12]]: Expressed displeasure with the party's methods at the [[Organisations/Pashae Institute|Pashae Institute]], emphasising the importance of maintaining diplomatic relations with benefactors. Oversaw the party's full membership approval (pending 50 gold fee) and assigned them the Mary Finsworth contract regarding stolen architectural plans from the [[Organisations/Cirrubi Central Bureaucracy|Cirrubi Central Bureaucracy]].

## Role

- Responsible for vetting new members and assigning contracts.
