## Background

Erhand Pashae is the head mage at the [[Pashae Institute]], an alchemical and magical research institution in [[Places/Continents/Jealeon/Regions/Aeremore/index|Aeremore]]. He is originally from [[Spuaburg]]. He left due when there was a change in power that lead to clear anti-magic sentiment in [[Spuaburg]].
