## Basic Information

- Species: Unknown (otherworldly entity)
- Physical description: Not corporeal; manifests as a whispering presence, often described as "always just behind" Threecie. No known physical form.
- Key relationships:
  - [[Threecie Novik|Threecie Novik]] (current potential warlock)
  - Other warlocks and entities opposed to necromancy

## Character Overview

- Class: Warlock Patron (Opposed to Necromancy)
- Personality traits: Manipulative, logical, persuasive, offers power in exchange for opposition to necromancy. Claims to operate "by the book" and is in good standing with her superiors.
- Notable equipment/companions: Communicates through a conjuration rock (artifact found in Session 14)

## Background

- Origins: Unknown; claims to be part of a group that uses souls as energy, but not in a way that affects the essence of the person. Opposed to necromancers who disrupt this balance.
- Goals: To recruit warlocks who will help stop necromancers and maintain the balance between life and death.
- Connections to other characters:
  - [[Threecie Novik|Threecie Novik]] (offered a pact)
  - [[Snadneg]] (previous warlock, manipulated to attack necromancer targets)

## Recent Developments

- [[Notes/Session 14|Session 14]]: Contacted Threecie via conjuration rock, offered a pact, explained her opposition to necromancy, and revealed the risks of Threecie's current patron operating "under the table."

## Investigation Notes

- Vicky's true nature and hierarchy remain mysterious.
- Her opposition to necromancy may have broader implications for the party's quest and magical politics.
- The conjuration rock serves as a physical tether for communication with her.
