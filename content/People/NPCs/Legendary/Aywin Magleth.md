## Background

[[People/NPCs/Legendary/Aywin Magleth|Aywin Magleth]] is a legendary elven wizard of immense power whose reputation precedes him throughout the known world. He operates from the shadows, maintaining a network of contacts and illusions to further his goals while avoiding direct confrontation with established powers.

Magleth has been observing [[Organisations/Adventuring Parties/The Prime Suspects|The Prime Suspects]] since their conflict with [[Organisations/Adventuring Parties/Smoke and Ropes|Smoke and Ropes]], recognising their potential as allies who lack rigid ties to the Pentaturres system. Someone has been using the party's likenesses to steal architectural plans of his manor from the [[Organisations/Cirrubi Central Bureaucracy|Cirrubi Central Bureaucracy]], though he claims no responsibility for this deception.

His methods are invasive and thorough. He prefers to explore people's minds directly rather than trust their words, viewing psychological examination as both more reliable and "more fun" than conventional interrogation.

## Abilities & Methods

- **Mind Magic:** Can render multiple people unconscious with a gesture and explore their psyches in detail
- **Illusion Mastery:** Maintains complex illusions like [[People/NPCs/Aeremore/The Cobbles/Mary Finsworth|Mary Finsworth]] as contact points
- **Teleportation:** Can transport groups across vast distances instantly
- **Memory Modification:** Capable of casting modify memory spells
- **Psychological Analysis:** Skilled at reading motivations, desires, and hidden aspects of personality

## Connections & Goals

Magleth seeks allies who can act independently against the Pentaturres without being bound by their diplomatic constraints. He's particularly interested in:

- [[People/Party/Robin Thaleia|Robin]]'s search for something from his past
- [[People/Party/Gralstak|Gralstak]]'s desire for revenge and need to "fill the void"
- [[People/Party/Lyra Shadowflame|Lyra]]'s struggle for freedom from parental restrictions
- [[People/Party/Manmosu|Manmosu]]'s conflicted emotions and mining interests
- [[People/Party/Lharamit Pirevin Rhyne|Lharamit]]'s doubts about her family and forced allegiance to the [[Cathedral]]
- [[People/Party/Threecie Novik|Threecie]]'s connection to a mysterious order and her necrotic condition
