## Rainia, the Winged Harmoniser

**Rainia, the Winged Harmoniser** is a deity of the Peace Domain, worshipped primarily in [[Spuaburg]]. She represents harmony, tranquility, and peaceful resolution of conflicts.

### Divine Attributes

- **Domain:** Peace
- **Region:** Associated with [[Spuaburg]]
- **Faction:** The Peace Keepers

### Regional Context

[[Spuaburg]] (associated with Rainia) was recently in conflict with [[Loshium]] (associated with [[Zurasus]]), though this was described as "not a religious war," as it was a war for access to the canal that connects [[Jealeon]] to the ocean.
